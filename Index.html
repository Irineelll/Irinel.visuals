ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Irinel Nicolas, a 17-year-old aspiring automotive photographer.
A lifelong petrolhead, I've been passionate about cars since birth. In 2019, I began combining that passion with photography through carspotting, and by 2022—at just 14 years old—I shot my first car features.

In 2024 I took things to the next level by turning photography into a profession. I'm currently collaborating with brands such as BMW Apan Motors, Dropdown, ShiftAndMeet, and more.
I'm open to both private and magazine shoots, available for projects anywhere across the country.
Have questions or want to book a shoot?
Feel free to reach out via email—I'd love to connect!
